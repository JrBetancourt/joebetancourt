Ranch High School Aviation Program Located in Las Vegas, NV

Ever since I can remember I have been fasinated in flying. I was amongst 100 out of 27,453 students to get accepted into the Rancho Aviation Program in 2006. I passed aerospace engineering, fundamentals of flight and rocketry. Passed my ground school test as well as graduated with 3.85 GPA. Unfortunately I was sick during my senior year which prevented me from achieving my VFR rating.
